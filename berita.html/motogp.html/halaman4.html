Dovizioso : The attandance king

The Italian,along with teammate Jorge Lorenz,has been competing on the world stage for 16 straight years-the third highest on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-years-old,who in the last two years has been - on paper- Marc Marquez's (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
